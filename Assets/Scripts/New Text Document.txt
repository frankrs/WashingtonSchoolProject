<b>Objective</b>
The objective of this lesson is to learn about our solar system.


<b>Facts that will be given are:</b>
- How many planets there are in our solar system.
- The names of the planets.
- What the planets are made of.
- The historical information regarding the names of the planets and more.
- We will also learn about the 2 astroid belts that are our neighbors and the bodies that live within the astroid fields.

Sky charts interaction - VR interaction - Change page interaction
